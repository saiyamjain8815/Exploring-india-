Jaipur - The Pink City

"A blend of heritage and royalty"

Jaipur, the capital of Rajasthan, is famous for its majestic palaces, forts, and vibrant culture. This city was founded in 1727 and is known as the 'Pink City' because of the color of the buildings in the old part of town.

Key attractions include the City Palace, Hawa Mahal, and Amer Fort. The architecture reflects the grandeur of Rajasthan's royal history. Jaipur is also part of the Golden Triangle tourist circuit.

Highlights:
  • Amer Fort: A sprawling fort known for its artistic style and the famous Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace).
  • Hawa Mahal: The Palace of Winds with intricate honeycomb design.
  • City Palace: A complex of palaces and courtyards that blend Mughal and Rajasthani architecture.
  • Jantar Mantar: A UNESCO World Heritage Site with astronomical instruments.
