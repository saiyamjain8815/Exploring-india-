Munnar, Kerala

"God's Own Country"

Munnar is a town in the Western Ghats mountain range in India‚Äôs Kerala state. A hill station and former resort for the British Raj elite, it's surrounded by rolling hills dotted with tea plantations established in the late 19th century. Eravikulam National Park, a habitat for the endangered mountain goat Nilgiri tahr, is home to the Lakkam Waterfalls, hiking trails and 2,695m-tall Anamudi Peak.

Highlights:
  • Tea Plantations: Explore the extensive tea gardens of Munnar.
  • Eravikulam National Park: Home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr.
  • Neelakurinji Bloom: A rare phenomenon occurring once in 12 years.
  • Mattupetty Dam: Enjoy a scenic boat ride.
